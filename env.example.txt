Copy the lines below into a new file named  .env.local  to enable automatic
order emails. Until you do, orders still work — the form falls back to opening
the customer's email app addressed to Kami.

  # Get a free key at https://resend.com
  RESEND_API_KEY=

  # Optional: a verified sending address.
  ORDER_FROM_EMAIL="Wreath Whimsy <orders@yourdomain.com>"
